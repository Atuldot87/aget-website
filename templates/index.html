Who We Are

A Trust Born from the Desire to Serve

Asharfi Global Educational Trust (AGET) is a registered public charitable trust established with an unwavering mission: to ensure that no child in Bihar — no matter their caste, gender, religion, or economic background — is denied the right to quality education.

Founded and driven by the vision of its settler, AGET has set forth on a journey to build schools, sponsor scholarships, establish coaching centres for competitive examinations, support orphans and widows, and create dignified opportunities for the physically and mentally challenged.

Registered under the District Registry Office, Saharsa, Bihar, AGET operates with complete transparency and is committed to channelling every rupee of public trust toward meaningful, measurable impact.
