Mission, Vision & Values

🎯 Our Mission

To establish, support, and sustain world-class educational institutions, scholarships, and welfare programs that empower every individual — regardless of caste, gender, religion, or economic standing — to achieve their full potential.

🔭 Our Vision

A Bihar — and an India — where no child is denied education due to poverty or circumstance, and where every community has access to knowledge, skills, and opportunity.

💡 Our Values

Transparency, Compassion, Inclusivity, Excellence, and Community. We govern ourselves with complete accountability to our donors, beneficiaries, and society.

Our Beginning

The Story of How AGET Began

"Education is not a privilege reserved for the few. It is the right of every child, every woman, every human being — and it was with this conviction that the Asharfi Global Educational Trust was born."

The trust was established in 2026 by its Settler, with a heartfelt desire to give back to the communities of Bihar. Growing up witnessing the struggle of talented children who could not access quality education due to financial barriers, the founders resolved to create a permanent institution that would serve generations to come.

Registered with the District Registry Office, Saharsa, AGET began its operations with a clear mandate: to make education accessible, affordable, and transformative for every deserving student across Bihar and India.
