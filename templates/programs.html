Scholarships & Academic Aid

AGET's flagship scholarship program ensures that financial barriers never become a reason for a child to drop out of school. We provide merit and need-based financial support, covering tuition, books, uniforms, and examination fees.

Our commitment extends without any discrimination of caste, color, race, creed, or sex — because talent knows no boundary.

  • ✓ Merit-based & need-based awards
  • ✓ Books, uniforms & stationery support
  • ✓ Stipends & monthly allowances
  • ✓ Medals & achievement recognition

Schools, Colleges & Coaching Centres

AGET establishes and supports schools, colleges, coaching centres for competitive examinations (IIT, NEET, UPSC, SSC, and more), professional training institutes, libraries, and research centres.

By creating these institutions, we ensure that students from rural Bihar have the same competitive opportunities as those in major cities.

💼

Vocational & Professional Training

Not every path to prosperity runs through a university. AGET's vocational training programs equip youth with practical, market-relevant skills — from computer literacy and tailoring to electronics and hospitality.

These programs create self-reliant individuals who can build sustainable livelihoods for themselves and their families.

Women, Widow & Senior Welfare

AGET recognises that orphans, widows, and aged persons often fall through the cracks of the social safety net. We establish institutions specifically designed to educate and rehabilitate these vulnerable groups.

Our mission: no widow without dignity, no orphan without a future, no elder without care.

👩‍🦽

Special Needs Education

We believe that physical or mental challenges should never be a barrier to education or a dignified life. AGET establishes and funds institutions that provide education, food, clothing, and holistic care to the differently-abled.

These centres are staffed with trained professionals and are equipped to address diverse learning needs with compassion and expertise.

Arts, Culture & Sports

AGET promotes the all-round development of students by supporting institutions for music, drama, fine arts, literature, and science. We also fund parks, gymnasiums, and sports clubs that develop physical fitness and team spirit.

Because education is not just academic — it's the development of the whole human being.
